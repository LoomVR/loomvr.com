Learn with Loom

Loom is a desktop application that allows user to learn a language in an imersive environment. Users can verbally speak with characters, play games, explore our town and much much more!


Built for Vr

We focused our development to prepare for the launch of major vr headsets. We believe that a fully immersive experience is the quickest way to learn any language and we want to place
