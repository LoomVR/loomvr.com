Virtual Reality Language Learning

What is Loom?
What is Loom?close

Explore a virtual Spanish town and speak with native Spaniards, while trying to build a life for yourself in Spain. Play mini games to earn money and strengthen your vocabulary. Get lost in the immersive experience, and live out your dreams of visiting a foreign country while becoming fluent in Spanish.

Education through Immersion

Education through Immersionclose

Loom VR is lightyears ahead of contemporary language learning programs. Why make language learning a chore? Why pay hundreds of dollars for a program filled with glorified index cards? Loom VR is a real world, real time, language learning experience that revolutionizes the learning process.

Why VR?
Why VR?close

Loom VR is an immersive language learning environment that enables you to learn at a faster pace than any other program on the market. The best way to learn a language is to immerse yourself entirely in the language through sight, sound, and speech.
